    nncli-favorite(1)¶

    Synopsis¶

    nncli –key|-k KEY favorite|unfavorite

    Description¶

    Favorite (or unfavorite) the note specified by KEY.

    Options¶

    --key, -k¶

    The ID of the note to operate on.
